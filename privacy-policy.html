1. Overview

Ink & Archives ("we," "us," or "our") operates inkandarchives.com, a digital bookstore selling public domain literary works in ePub and PDF format. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the site.

2. Information We Collect

Information you give us directly

  • Account & checkout details — name, email address, and billing information when you create an account or make a purchase. Payment card details are collected and processed by our payment provider, Stripe, and are never stored on our own servers.
  • Newsletter signup — your email address, if you choose to subscribe.
  • Communications — anything you send us by email or through the site's chat assistant.

Information collected automatically

  • Local device storage — your shopping cart, wishlist, and reading-progress tracker are stored in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us unless you complete a purchase.
  • Usage data — pages viewed, approximate location (derived from browser timezone/IP), device and browser type, and referring site.

Information from third parties

When you complete a purchase, our payment processor (Stripe) shares limited transaction details with us, such as confirmation that payment succeeded and the email address used for checkout.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information to data brokers or third-party advertisers. We share information only as follows:

  • Stripe (payment processing) — handles your billing details directly; we do not see or store full card numbers.
  • Email delivery providers (e.g. Mailgun) — used solely to send order confirmations and, if you opted in, newsletters.
  • Service providers who help us run the site (hosting, analytics) under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — if required to comply with a law, court order, or to protect our rights.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use browser local storage (not traditional cookies) for cart, wishlist, reading-progress, and privacy-preference data. This is essential to the site working correctly and cannot be disabled without breaking core shopping features.

If analytics tools are enabled, they may use cookies or similar identifiers to understand aggregate site usage. You can control this through the "Manage Choices" privacy banner on the site.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time (unsubscribe link in every email)
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information, where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 13.

7. California & US State Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and similar laws in other US states may give you additional rights, including the right to:

  • Know what categories of personal information we collect and why
  • Request deletion of personal information we hold about you
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

We do not knowingly sell personal information in the traditional sense. To the extent any data sharing with service providers could be considered "sharing" under CPRA, you can opt out at any time using the "Manage Choices" link in the privacy banner shown on this site, or by emailing us directly.

8. Global Privacy Control

This site honors the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser or a browser extension sends a GPC signal, we automatically treat this as a valid opt-out request for the sale/sharing of your personal information, with no further action required from you.

9. Data Retention

We retain account and order information for as long as needed to fulfil orders, comply with tax and accounting obligations, and resolve disputes. Newsletter email addresses are kept until you unsubscribe. Local browser storage (cart, wishlist, reading tracker) remains on your device until you clear your browser data or it expires.

10. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted payment processing through Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant) and secure connections (HTTPS) across the site. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
